## Decisions
- Keep retrieval backends such as QMD supported and visible without treating them as the primary definition of agent memory.
- Keep the human contract simple: visual explanation, evidence trail, and source-of-truth references.
- Treat lexical-only retrieval as degraded but still operational; missing vectors should not invalidate the whole memory surface.
- Do not let the repo claim full health from note-link density alone; proof has to include the actual reopening and retrieval path.

## Constraints / First Principles
- Context Spine must remain inspectable without a special runtime.
- Durable truth should still resolve through files, not hidden runtime state.
- Visual explainers are the required human-facing feature when the system shape is easier to absorb spatially than through prose.
