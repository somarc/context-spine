- qmd:
  - qmd://context-spine-meta/context-spine/spine-notes-context-spine.md
- action: Keep the repo-level memory explicit that partial hydration can come from QMD runtime selection rather than missing collections or missing sqlite-vec binaries.
- files:
  - meta/context-spine/sessions/2026-03-17-session.md
- details:
  - The installed sqlite-vec extension loads under Node, but the qmd launcher selects Bun whenever BUN_INSTALL is set. Under Bun on this macOS host, bun:sqlite cannot load dynamic extensions unless QMD first points Bun at a Homebrew SQLite dylib via Database.setCustomSQLite(...). That leaves lexical update/bootstrap green while context:embed fails.
